# AI Strategy

Do not start by training a custom model.

Start with:

1. Clean operational data
2. Rule-based risk scoring
3. AI-generated structured insights
4. Embeddings for SOPs, notes, incidents, and audits
5. Manager feedback
6. Later predictive model training

## First AI insights

- Locations with declining compliance
- Overdue corrective actions
- Repeated failed checklist questions
- Food safety exceptions
- Missing proof/evidence
- Launch readiness risk
- High incident recurrence

## Future predictive models

After enough historical data:

- Probability of failed audit
- Probability of overdue corrective action
- Location compliance forecast
- Staff training risk
- Incident recurrence risk
